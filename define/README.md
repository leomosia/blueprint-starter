# Define

Define is where a piece of work becomes clear before movement begins.

Use this folder to capture the purpose, boundaries, success criteria, constraints, and reason behind the work. The goal is not to over-plan. The goal is to know what is being built, changed, studied, or pursued before energy is spent executing it.

Good definition answers:

- What is this?
- Why does it matter?
- What is in scope?
- What is out of scope?
- What would make this successful?
