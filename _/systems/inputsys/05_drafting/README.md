# drafting/

## Step 5 — Drafting & Refinement

### What this folder is

`drafting/` is where transformed material becomes **coherent written form**.

This is not exploration.
This is not final output.
This is **deliberate construction under momentum**.

Drafts here are allowed to be imperfect.
They are not allowed to be vague.

## Core Principle

**Momentum first. Precision second.**

Drafting exists to get ideas out of your head and onto the page without killing velocity.

## What happens in Drafting

### 1. Initial Drafting

This phase creates **shape**.

- **Expanding Notes**
  Take transformed artifacts and expand them into full drafts.
  - ideas become paragraphs
  - frameworks become sections
  - outlines become narratives

- **Idea Articulation**
  Focus on:
  - logical flow
  - internal coherence
  - explicit reasoning

  If a thought cannot be written clearly, it is not ready.

- **Momentum Maintenance**
  No polishing.
  No style obsession.
  No premature editing.

  Finish the draft.

### 2. Depth & Coherence Enhancement

This phase strengthens **substance**.

- **Goal Alignment**
  Ensure the draft aligns with:
  - core principles
  - fieldnotes
  - strategic intent
  - audience purpose

  Drift is corrected here.

- **Insight Addition**
  Add:
  - missing arguments
  - necessary context
  - counterpoints

  Remove repetition and filler.

- **Consistency Checks**
  Enforce:
  - consistent terminology
  - stable tone
  - coherent structure

  Sloppiness erodes trust.

### 3. Rewriting & Refinement

This phase sharpens **expression**.

- **Clarification & Precision**
  Rewrite until:
  - sentences carry one idea
  - concepts are unambiguous
  - language is exact

- **Argument Strengthening**
  Reinforce claims with:
  - evidence
  - examples
  - reasoning chains

  Assertions must earn their place.

- **Narrative Flow Improvement**
  Improve transitions, pacing, and progression.
  The reader should never feel lost.

## What does NOT belong here

- Raw notes
- Analytical exploration
- Final publication files
- Emotional journaling

If it’s finished, it moves on.
If it’s unclear, it goes back upstream.

## Exit Rule (Non-Negotiable)

A draft leaves `drafting/` only when it is:

1. Coherent end-to-end
2. Aligned with intent
3. Structurally sound

Perfection is not required. Readiness is.

## Mental Model

- **Transformation** → usable structure
- **Drafting** → coherent form
- **Output** → public or operational release

Drafting is where thinking becomes readable.
