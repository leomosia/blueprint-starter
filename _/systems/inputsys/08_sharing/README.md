# sharing/

## Step 8 — Sharing & Teaching

### What this folder is

`sharing/` is where knowledge **leaves your head and enters the world**.

This is not self-expression.
This is **intentional transmission**.

Teaching is the final test of understanding.

## Core Principle

**If you can’t teach it clearly, you don’t understand it deeply enough.**

Sharing exists to scale insight beyond the individual mind.

## What happens in Sharing

### 1. Distributing Knowledge to the Right Channels

This phase ensures **correct placement**.

- **Writing & Publishing**
  Publish insights as:

  - blog posts
  - essays
  - internal memos
  - public letters

  Writing sharpens thinking.

- **Company Documentation**
  Integrate learnings into:

  - internal systems
  - operating manuals
  - cultural doctrine

  Knowledge must survive turnover.

- **Social & Public Distribution**
  Share distilled insights through:

  - social platforms
  - talks
  - interviews

  Reach matters when intent is clear.

### 2. Tailoring Information for Maximum Impact

This phase optimizes **signal delivery**.

- **Audience-Specific Formatting**
  Adapt depth and language for:

  - internal teams
  - public readers
  - students
  - peers

  Same truth, different packaging.

- **Actionable & Practical Framing**
  Shared knowledge must answer:

  - what to do
  - how to think
  - what to avoid

  Inspiration without application is noise.

- **Clarity & Refinement Through Teaching**
  Teaching exposes:

  - weak explanations
  - hidden assumptions
  - conceptual gaps

  Confusion is diagnostic.

### 3. Strengthening Learning Through Teaching

This phase locks knowledge in place.

- **Journals & Reflective Writing**
  Teaching feeds back into:

  - personal understanding
  - long-term clarity
  - intellectual honesty

- **Training & Onboarding Material**
  Codify systems so others can:

  - execute
  - decide
  - lead without you

  This is real leverage.

- **Iterative Feedback Loops**
  Capture questions, objections, and misunderstandings.
  Refine the material upstream.

  The audience becomes a stress test.

## What does NOT belong here

- Unverified ideas
- Half-formed thinking
- Private processing
- Raw drafts

If it’s fragile, keep it private.

## Exit Rule (Non-Negotiable)

Every shared artifact must result in at least one of:

1. Understanding by others
2. Adoption or use
3. Meaningful feedback
4. Refinement of the original idea

Broadcast without impact is vanity.

## Mental Model

- **Utilization** → proof
- **Sharing** → scale
- **Teaching** → mastery

Sharing is how intelligence compounds.
